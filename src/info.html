That's not me in the mosh pit.

I'm a Creative Director. Writer. And More.

I've helped a President help young men help each other, named a credit card still running 16 years later, and launched two Samsung campaigns that went global. I conspired with Dracula to kill flash photography. And convinced a generation that pen-spinning is focus. I write manifestos. One got me hired on the spot.

Pre-med and poetry at Cornell. Mentored by A.R. Ammons. He taught me that science and art aren't that far apart.

Born in Nicaragua's highlands. Grown under Miami Beach's sun. Forged in New York.

Now operating globally as Meanwhile In Jupiter.
