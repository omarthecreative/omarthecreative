Full Activation Case Study

THE CHALLENGE

Stoli was launching a limited edition bottle — Night Edition. But limited editions are forgettable. They live on shelves for a season and disappear. We needed to make Night Edition feel like more than a SKU. We needed to make it feel like a movement.

THE INSIGHT

The bottle glows in the dark. That's not a feature. That's a philosophy. Night isn't when the party ends. It's when certain people come alive. The ones who see better in darkness. Hunt at night. Love the night. We weren't launching a bottle. We were launching a tribe.

THE WORK

We created a campaign that shines brightest when the sun goes down. Glow-in-the-dark everything — the bottle, POS, print, OOH, and experiential. Billboards that revealed hidden messages after dark. For high-end nightlife, we created limited edition bottle holders with UV lamps so barstaff could charge up the bottle as they brought it to your table. The ritual became part of the product. Every touchpoint designed for the night.

THE IMPACT

A limited edition that refused to stay limited. Night Edition was so successful in Year 1 that it spawned Years 2 and 3 — with sales increasing 350% and Night Edition accounting for 60% of that growth. The platform eventually evolved into elit Night Edition, the ultra-luxury LED-illuminated bottle. What launched as a seasonal SKU became a permanent franchise.
