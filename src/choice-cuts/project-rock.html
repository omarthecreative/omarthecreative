THE CHALLENGE

Project Rock belonged to The Rock. His gym. His ritual. His audience. Under Armour wanted to open the doors — to women, to kids, to anyone willing to work. The question wasn't who could wear it. The question was who deserved to earn it.

THE INSIGHT

Iron builds muscle. But what builds champions? Not equipment. Not genetics. Belief. Drive. Refusal to quit when your body begs you to stop. That's not iron. That's iron will.

THE WORK

We named it The Iron Will Collection. Wrote a manifesto that became the anthem. Found the proof in four athletes: The Rock, Lindsey Vonn, Flash (age 8), and Jojo (age 11). Vertical videos on Instagram Stories. No scripts. No sets. Just sweat, chalk, and truth.

THE IMPACT

Every product sold out. 100+ million views on Instagram. An iron will dropped in all sizes.
